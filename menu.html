Future Vision of Victory

VICTORY has very clearly defined future plans as under:
To move to own well designed premises to accommodate 10000 Homeless people - FREE OF COST
To provide in-house medical facilities and physiotherapy
To provide a place for prayer, yoga, meditation, recreation, library and other needs.
To develop organic kitchen garden to make VICTORY self sufficient.

Our Mission

The mission of Victory Home is to 'Serve the living temple of God' by attending their needs, wounds and agony at the dusk-time of their life given by their own parents and childrens.

Developing infrastructure, manpower, research, training and integrated facilities for the ageing population.

Victory is committed to provide affordable, creative and innovative solutions which bridge housing and health care of Homeless peoples.
